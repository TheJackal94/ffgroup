FF Gas: Clean Energy, Delivered Intelligently

A 100% female-owned business pioneering wholesale LPG solutions for the commercial and agricultural sectors.

A Modern Approach to LPG Supply

Launched in 2022, FF Gas is the latest innovation from the FF Group, focusing on the wholesale LPG needs of Limpopo, Gauteng, and Mpumalanga. Our integrated model includes transport, installation, and maintenance, giving us full control over the logistic chain to guarantee consistent, excellent service.

Our Smart Supply System

  • Mini-Bulk Installation: We install our own mini-bulk tanks at your site, tailored to your needs.
  • Remote Stock Monitoring: All tanks are fitted with advanced meters, allowing us to remotely monitor your consumption and tank levels to plan proactive deliveries. You never have to worry about running out of product.
  • Diverse Sourcing: We are not reliant on a single supplier. With certifications to load from Sasol, Richards Bay, and Matole in Mozambique, we ensure a constant supply of LPG.
